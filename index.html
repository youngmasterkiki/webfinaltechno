A Tree and Plant Naming Platform for a Sustainable Tomorrow

Let us discuss together! +62 853-9534-3624

the pioneer of Digital Collaboration with Nature program in Institute Technology PLN

The problem we want to solve is the lack of awareness and involvement of the community towards trees and plants in a specific scope (such as the ITPLN park environment). We aim to inspire a sense of ownership, appreciation, and care for nature amidst the urban environment.

We have chosen a startup idea titled GreenGengs, where we plan to develop a Go-Green oriented program. Our goal is to design a platform that provides information on naming, as well as guidance on how to care for and maintain each tree and plant within a limited scope, such as the campus park of the Institute Technology PLN Jakarta.
